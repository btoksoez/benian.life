Description

Together with my co-founder, we created a reflection guide for microdosing psilocybin. After 1 year of development, our product was a package that included a 100+ page guidebook with daily reflection questions, a 1-month supply of psilocybin truffles, a scale, and a pen. I was mainly responsible for all administrative tasks, representation of the company at pitches, and the design of the website and book cover. I decided to step out due to legal hurdles and a shift in personal interests.
