Description

PurposeFlow is a regenerative community in Portugal, with multiple volunteers incoming every week. Visit their website to learn more about their mission. I streamlined their onboarding process for new volunteers. Previously, the community relied on manually adapted emails to communicate essential information both before and after a volunteer's arrival. The goal was to automate this process for improved efficiency.

Using Google Apps Scripts, I developed a fully automated system that manages the entire process—from data collection via forms to personalized email communication. The system adapts the content dynamically based on the data provided by the volunteers, ensuring that each person receives the relevant information, reducing manual labor, and enhancing the onboarding experience.
